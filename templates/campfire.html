Campfire Username

Since you are 13 or older, you can link your Campfire Username. This will be used for awarding your check-in stamps, and linking your profile to events and community features.

To find your username, open the Campfire App, tap your avatar in the bottom right corner, and look for the name after the @ symbol.

🔥 Don’t have Campfire yet? Download it from your app store and create a username to link later.
